Angelic


Dancing dark in winter skies

Curving hills aloft creation

Converge a brook run down her spine

A melody met in her words and dictation

The moon shone soft above Willand Pond

and the smokey topaz in her eyes danced with temptation

Angelic demeanor from somewhere beyond, a world without explanation

Steady my hand and light my fire visit me in dreams and my desires

One dream I stood by your funeral pyre as hymns rang out from a colored choir

While cardinals race toward the sky you'll return with wings raised high

Damned to earth, I’m not sure why but for now you're here

My Angel. Dancing dark in winter skies.
